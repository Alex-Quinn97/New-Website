Everything from large-scale event marketing and merchandise design to everyday, one-off projects. Highlighted are four (4) of the bigger projects that I worked on that are not visible in other areas in my portfolio. These projects also span across the time period before and after OU's rebranding in the summer of 2023 which can be seen when browsing each individual project as well as "everything else" that was designed during my time at the Graduate College.

  • A brand new event concept, GradCon was born out of a donation to the college with the goal of “improving the graduate student experience”. Recalling my first experience attending Adobe MAX and older experiences with Comic-Con, I had the idea to host a similar event for incoming graduate students as a mechanism to provide information, improve the overall onboarding experience, and bring an in-person, community-building experience that the students at the university were in desperate need of (according to previous GradSERU surveys and polls).

    BUT WITH SO MANY EVENTS HAPPENING, AND EACH EVENT USING THE SAME BRANDING, HOW CAN YOU STAND OUT?

    Changing Adapting the Brand

    OU’s new branding places a lot of emphasis on iconography inspired by small details in the brickwork and overall Cherokee gothic architecture around campus.

    I wanted to focus on emphasizing this and create somewhat of an “iteration” or further exploration of using this style of design.
