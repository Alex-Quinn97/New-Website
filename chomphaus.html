Inspired by a misreading of a Freddy's Burger advertisement, ChompHaus is a semi-regular, esoteric, themed dinner party that I host. I love to cook and sharing that love with others, so I decided to create a dinner party that asks guests to choose a part of a dinner to bring and cook everything at my apartment.

Each item must adhere to that party's theme and a winner is voted on at the end of the night to be declared the newest "Chompion" and they get to "spin the smorgasboard" to decide the next theme. Yes, they are awarded the "Chompion's Belt".
